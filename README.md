# 🔥 Project Forge

> Building a premium digital platform and reusable design system for modern business solutions.

## Overview

Project Forge is the personal digital platform of **Dilshan Peiris**, designed to establish a professional web presence while serving as the foundation for future digital products, business websites, and SaaS applications.

The project is built around **Forge UI**, a reusable design engineering system that powers every application within the Forge ecosystem.

---

## Vision

Project Forge is more than a portfolio.

It is a scalable platform that:

- Showcases professional experience
- Demonstrates technical capabilities
- Generates qualified business leads
- Hosts case studies and insights
- Provides business consultation tools
- Serves as the foundation for future ventures
